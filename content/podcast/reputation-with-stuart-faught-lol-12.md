Reputation isn't just a buzzword -- it's your most valuable asset. In this episode of Lords of Lending, hosts Shane Pierson, Stephanie Dunn, and Brian Congelliere are joined by special guest Stuart Faught, founder of Online Reputation Pros. Together, they explore how reputation impacts every stage of your business journey -- from gaining trust with lenders and sellers to attracting customers and scaling operations.

Stuart unpacks the real mechanics behind online reviews, credibility, and how perception shapes opportunity. This isn't about gaming the system -- it's about aligning your digital and real-world presence with who you actually are as an operator.

They dive deep into:

- The disconnect between actual experience and online perception
- Reputation as leverage during SBA deals and acquisitions
- Strategic timing for review requests
- Tools and frameworks for building sustainable, authentic reputations

Whether you're a first-time buyer, seasoned operator, or advisor in the space, this episode delivers tactical insight and mindset shifts that can improve how others see -- and trust -- your business.
